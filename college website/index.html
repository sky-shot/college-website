Courses we offer

All Undergraduate and Postgraduate Programs are affiliated to
J.C Bose University of Science and Technology, YMCA (Faridabad)

Bachelor of Technology

It is a 4-year undergraduate course divided into 8 semesters. BTech courses encompass a broad spectrum of engineering disciplines, ranging from traditional fields like civil and mechanical engineering to emerging domains like artificial intelligence and robotics. Students can choose multiple specializations like Mechanical, Electrical, AI & ML, etc with BTech CSE being the most popular.

Bachelor of Computer Application

BCA is short for Bachelor of Computer Application. It is a 3 year UG course that trains students in Operating Systems, Programming Languages, Computer Networks, etc. Students can get job opportunities in the IT sector after completing the BCA course.

EXPLORE

Bachelor of Business Administration

BBA is a 3-year undergraduate program that focuses on business and management principles. BBA course spans across 6 semesters, covering areas like finance, marketing, human resources, and entrepreneurship. BBA is the most sought-after undergraduate degree after class 12, ideal for candidates aspiring to pursue careers in sales, marketing, HR, and finance.

EXPLORE

Master of Business Administration

MBA is a 2-year postgraduate program that focuses on developing skills and knowledge in the fields of business and management. MBA is the most sought-after postgraduate degree, ideal for candidates aspiring for senior leadership and managerial roles.
